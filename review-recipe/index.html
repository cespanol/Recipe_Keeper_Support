App Review Lemon Toast

This small public recipe exists so App Review can exercise Recipe Keeper’s Paste Link workflow without using private user content.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 2 tablespoons ricotta
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Instructions

  1. Toast the sourdough until golden.
  2. Spread with ricotta, then finish with lemon zest and honey.
